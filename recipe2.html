Aloo Bharta

Ingredients:

  • 3 boiled potatoes
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mash the boiled potatoes.
  2. Add chopped onions, green chilies, and salt.
  3. Pour mustard oil and mix thoroughly.
  4. Serve with warm rice.
