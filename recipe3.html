Beguni

Ingredients:

  • 1 large eggplant (thinly sliced)
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix gram flour, turmeric, chili powder, and salt with water to make a smooth batter.
  2. Dip eggplant slices into the batter.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with rice or as a snack.
