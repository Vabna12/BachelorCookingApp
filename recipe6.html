Chingri Bhuna

Ingredients:

  • 300g shrimp (cleaned)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (sliced)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and fry onions until soft.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add turmeric, chili powder, salt, and green chilies. Cook well.
  4. Add shrimp and stir fry for 5-7 minutes until cooked.
  5. Serve hot with rice.
