Egg Bhuna

Ingredients:

  • 4 boiled eggs
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (sliced)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt as needed
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and fry onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until soft.
  4. Add boiled eggs and green chilies. Mix gently.
  5. Cook for 5 minutes. Serve hot with rice or paratha.
