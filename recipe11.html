Shorshe Ilish

Ingredients:

  • 4 pieces hilsa fish
  • 2 tbsp mustard paste
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp mustard oil

Instructions:

  1. Rub turmeric and salt on fish pieces.
  2. Heat mustard oil and add mustard paste and green chilies.
  3. Add fish pieces and cook gently for 8-10 minutes.
  4. Serve with steamed rice.
