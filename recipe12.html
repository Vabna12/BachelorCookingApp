Piyaju

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils (soaked and ground)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix ground lentils with onions, chilies, cumin, and salt.
  2. Form small balls and flatten slightly.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Serve as a snack or with tea.
