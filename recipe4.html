Dal Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup lentils (moong or masoor dal)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 green chilies (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Boil lentils with salt and turmeric until soft.
  2. In another pan, heat oil and fry onions and chilies.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Pour the cooked lentils into the pan and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice or roti.
