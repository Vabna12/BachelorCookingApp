Vegetable Khichuri

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup lentils (moong or masoor dal)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (potato, carrot, beans)
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Fry onions in oil until golden.
  2. Add vegetables and spices. Stir well.
  3. Add washed rice and lentils. Mix.
  4. Add 4 cups of water and cook until soft.
  5. Serve hot with pickle or fried egg.
