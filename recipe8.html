Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and scramble the eggs. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, fry vegetables until soft.
  3. Add cooked rice, soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add scrambled eggs and mix well. Serve hot.
